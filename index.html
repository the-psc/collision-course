Collision Course! 🛰️💥

Pieces of orbital debris are predicted to pass near your satellite. Your goal is to plan and carry out manoeuvres that prevent them from colliding.

Rules

  • The image shown will represent the different probabilities of collision resulting from manoeuvres of different sizes and directions at different times.
  • Each manouevre burns fuel. When you run out of fuel you will no longer be able to manoeuvre.

Controls

  • The dot represents your current plan for the manoeuvre. You can move the dot using the following controls
  • Up arrow / Down arrow → Adjust manoeuvre size and direction
  • Left arrow / Right arrow → Adjust manoeuvre timing
  • Space → Lock in manoeuvre plan
  • P → Pause game
  • Q → Quit game

Tips

  • Blue regions indicate low risk, yellow medium risk, and red high risk.
  • Set your planned manoeuvre time ahead of the NOW line. You can't plan a manoeuvre in the past.
  • Big manoeuvres conserve lower the risk of collision the most but use a lot of fuel.
  • Early manoeuvres typically lower the risk of collision more.
